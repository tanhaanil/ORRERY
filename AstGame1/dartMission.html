DART Mission: Defending Earth from Asteroids

The Double Asteroid Redirection Test (DART) mission, conducted by NASA, was the first-ever initiative aimed at demonstrating asteroid deflection through kinetic impact. Launched in November 2021, DART successfully collided with its target asteroid, Dimorphos, on September 26, 2022.
